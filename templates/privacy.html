Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information — your name, email address, and password (stored as a secure hash).
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data — basic logs such as login times, used solely for security and debugging.

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive financial account details.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to provide and improve the Spendly service:

  • To authenticate you and display your personal expense records.
  • To generate summaries and breakdowns within your account.
  • To communicate important service updates or security notices.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect it against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. Passwords are never stored in plain text.

You can delete your account at any time. Upon deletion, your personal information and expense records are permanently removed from our systems.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly uses a small number of third-party services to operate:

  • Google Fonts — fonts are loaded from Google's CDN. Google may collect basic request data per their own privacy policy.

We do not integrate with advertising networks, social media trackers, or analytics platforms that profile you across sites.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact us through the information provided on the site. We will respond as promptly as possible.
